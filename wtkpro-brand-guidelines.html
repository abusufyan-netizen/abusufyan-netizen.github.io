06

Voice & Tone

Technical — not jargony

Speak clearly to developers without dumbing things down. Use precise language but avoid unnecessary complexity.

Confident — not arrogant

State capabilities directly. "Zero data transmitted." Not "We try our best to keep your data safe."

Fast — not brief to the point of vague

Copy should be short and scannable. Every word earns its place. No filler phrases, no corporate speak.

Trustworthy — not preachy

Privacy and security are features, not sermons. Mention them once, clearly, and move on.

DO"Format JSON. Generate passwords. Encode data. All client-side."
DON'T"We are passionate about providing world-class developer tools that empower you to achieve your goals."
